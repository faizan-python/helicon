About Helicon Engineering Works

Established in the year 1984, we, “Helicon Engineering Works”, are a renowned manufacturer and supplier of Gear, Gear Boxes, Helical Pinion & Gear and Worm Shafts & Wheels in Vapi. Manufactured using quality tested metals and other raw material, our range matches well with the set industrial standards, such as DIN. These are appreciated for their tough construction, accurate functioning, high working efficiency and abrasion resistance. Catering to the diverse requirements of various automotive, mechanical and engineering industries, we offer these products with customization options.

With the assistance of our sound infrastructure facility and immensely qualified team of experts, we have been able to meet the standards of the market. It is the combined efforts of these two factors that all our business processes are accomplished within the committed time frame. Moreover, with our strict quality measures, we mange to eliminate all the flaws from our entire business process. It is owing to all these factors that we have been able to attain a prominent market position and a wide clientèle.

The visionary guidance of our mentor, 'Mr. Harun & Mr. Anish', has made us achieve our organizational goals of maximum client satisfaction. It is his domain expertise and in-depth knowledge that has made us move ahead in this industry as compared to our counterparts existing in the market.
